# Golden Gate v0.1 interpretation correction

Date: 2026-07-18

This note corrects the interpretation of the frozen Golden Gate v0.1 artifacts. It does not alter any v0.1 byte, hash, case, or result.

## Economic schedules

All 1,000,000 captive randomized schedules recorded in `v0.1/economic-invariant-record.json` terminated in `ROUTED`. They therefore did **not** establish broad terminal-state coverage, transition coverage, boundary coverage, fairness, or liveness. The retained claim is narrower:

> No declared invariant failed on the generated traces that were actually executed.

The v0.2 state-space model replaces trace volume as the primary evidence and reports reachable states, accepted and rejected transitions, guard boundaries, causes, terminal phases, fairness assumptions, and shortest witnesses separately.

## Blindness

The v0.1 hidden packet is `AUTHOR_KNOWN / PARTICIPANT_BLIND`. Its oracle was created under author control. It is not reviewer-originated blindness and supplies no independent attestation.

External evidence requires a frozen candidate, reviewer-originated hidden cases, reviewer-controlled 2-of-3 oracle custody, receipt-bound Bulla and clean-room submissions, and a post-submission reveal transcript.
