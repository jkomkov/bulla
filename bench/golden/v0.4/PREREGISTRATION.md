# Golden v0.4 Precedent Yield — Preregistration

Profile: `bulla.golden-suite/0.4-experimental`

Classification before execution: `INTERNAL_CAPTIVE`.

## Frozen design

- 240 cases: 12 semantic seam lineages, 20 cases per lineage.
- Per lineage: cases 0–15 are design; 16–19 are untouched holdout.
- Each lineage binds one planted FRSL-1 reason of at most 32 AST nodes,
  authority regime, closure warrant, applicability scope, semantic epoch, and
  case order.
- Findings are `CAPTIVE_MACHINE_DERIVED`; none are human adjudications.
- The earlier 126 partial cases are an instrumentation regression cohort, not a
  holdout.

The case schedule includes exact records, canonically equivalent records,
reason-preserving variations, irrelevant facts, a fresh reason, a scope edge,
an authority change, a closure change, conflict, and epoch supersession.

## Arms

Each frozen chronological and greedy schedule is evaluated under:

1. exact preclusion;
2. authorized `BINDING_WITHIN_SCOPE` precedent;
3. advisory label-only generalization, which never gates action;
4. the planted declared-rule upper bound.

The greedy order is computed from design metadata before the holdout is run.
No parameter is tuned against holdout outcomes.

## Metrics

After every authoritative act the report records RELY, REFUSE, and ESCALATE
mass; newly certified cases; exact and reason reuse; typed legislation, scope,
authority, closure, conflict, epoch, and resource exits; formula/proof size;
runtime and logical counters; and findings to 50%, 80%, and 90% eligible
coverage. Burden coordinates remain separate.

Marginal precedent yield is the number of additional future cases certified
beyond the finding's own case. It is not denominated as economic value.

## Frozen classification

- `DEMONSTRATED_COMPOUNDING`: zero unsafe transfer; authorized precedent beats
  exact preclusion on at least 9/12 holdout lineages; median marginal yield at
  least two; median findings to 80% coverage improves at least 25% on eligible
  lineages.
- `LIMITED_COMPOUNDING`: zero unsafe transfer and positive sub-threshold reuse.
- `FALSIFIED_AS_AMORTIZATION_ROUTE`: median yield at most one, failure to
  dominate on at least half the lineages, or routine dependence on legislation
  or new epochs.

Any unsafe transfer, refusal erasure, or cross-boundary reuse is a soundness
failure and blocks packet freeze.

## F11

Exactly 52 laundering cases cover 13 archetypes in direct, nested,
serialized-replay, and authority/epoch-mutation forms. The thirteenth is
strategic resource starvation. Required outputs are typed rejection,
`LEGISLATION_REQUIRED`, `TERM_STALE`, or `ROUTE`.

Mutation operators remove authority tokens, change precedent effects, forge
forum findings, replace categorical harm with reserve tiers, change epochs,
and truncate budget frontiers. Golden v0.1–v0.3 remain byte-frozen.
